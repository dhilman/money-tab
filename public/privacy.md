## MoneyTab Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 July 2024

## 1. Introduction

MoneyTab ("we", "our", or "us") is a Telegram Mini App (TMA) designed for managing personal and shared expenses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our service. By using MoneyTab, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information Provided by Telegram

When you access MoneyTab, Telegram provides us with certain information about you, which may include: user ID, name, username, language preference, and profile photo. We store this information to identify you and provide our service.

### 2.2 User-Provided Information

We collect and store information that you voluntarily provide while using MoneyTab, such as: expense and subscription details, group names, group members, and other information you choose to share.

### 2.3 Analytical Data

To improve our service, we may collect analytical data, which may include: device type, IP address, region. We do not use third-party tracking tools for this purpose.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
- To provide and maintain our service
- To identify users and manage accounts
- To improve and optimize our service
- To analyze usage patterns and trends

We adhere to two fundamental principles:
1. We do not use your data to show ads.
2. We only collect the data which is required to provide the service.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information, including encryption techniques and authentication procedures, among others, to maintain the security of your online session.

Your data is retained for as long as needed to provide the service. If you wish to request the deletion of your data, please contact us at privacy@moneytab.app.

## 5. Sharing of Information

MoneyTab allows users to make their expenses and profile information shareable. When you choose to share a link, other users with access to that link will be able to view the shared information (e.g., join an expense).

We do not share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

## 6. User Rights and Controls

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@moneytab.app.

## 7. Children's Privacy

MoneyTab is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age or minors. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@moneytab.app.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

## 9. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@moneytab.app.
